ABOUT WORKZONE

Staying concentrated and being productive is important, especially when working from home.
With many distractions and long workhours, it is easy to forget the most foundational needs.
WorkZone is an application which will help you turn your home office into the perfect environment for your workday,
by adapting to your personal needs and preferences.



Key features of the application

Sensors

With sensors to collect data about the conditions in the room, the application is able to gain knowledge about the current environment.

Reasoning

By using the data collected by the sensor, algorithms are able to process the data and make decisions based on the inputs.

Actuators

With actuators to perform changes, the application is able to act on the decisions made, and make changes to meet the users preferences.

Personalized

Each user has their own profile where they are able to emphasize their preferred conditions, to gain the best possible work environment.
